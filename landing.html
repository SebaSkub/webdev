Welcome to League of Legends

League of Legends is a popular online multiplayer battle arena (MOBA) game developed and published by Riot Games.

Here are some interesting facts about League of Legends:

  • It was officially released on October 27, 2009.
  • League of Legends has a large and active player community worldwide.
  • The game features over 150 champions, each with unique abilities and playstyles.
